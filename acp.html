Codingal is an online platform that teaches coding to school students through live, 1-on-1 classes.
It offers courses in programming languages like Python, Scratch, and HTML/CSS, tailored to different age groups and skill levels.
Codingal's curriculum is aligned with international standards and focuses on building logical thinking and problem-solving skills.
Students can participate in coding competitions and showcase their projects to gain confidence and recognition.
The platform aims to make coding fun and engaging by using interactive lessons and real-world projects.
